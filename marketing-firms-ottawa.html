Marketing -What is It?

In the simplest terms, marketing refers to promoting your products or services in the best possible way, thus enticing potential customers to buy your products. It is not just about putting advertisements out -marketing is a complex process that includes manythings. For example, selecting the target audience, designing the phrases, slogans, and images used, tying up with celebrities, and analyzing adverts' impact ispart of the marketing process.
