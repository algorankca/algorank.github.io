SEO in Ottawa: Types and techniques.

SEO is a quite vast process that includes various types and techniques. There are three types of SEO:

1) White Hat SEO

2) Black Hat SEO

3) Grey Hat SEO

What is White Hat Search Engine Optimization?

This SEO improves the ranking of your Website on the search engine by following all the policies of it. This SEO process will upload the original article, which will be according to ti the title. It involves the content for human readers.

White Hat SEO includes the following techniques:

a) Outreach: This consists of the creation of backlinks for the company's Website, which will improve organic visibility.

b) Content development with high quality: The content of your Website is unique and original.

c) Link acquisition campaigns supported by high-quality content: This process will actively try to increase links to the Website.

d) Restructuring of the Website: It is related to the organization of content.

e) Website optimization: It will increase the quantity and quality of the traffic.

What is Black Hat Search Engine Optimization?

The black hat SEO does not follow any policies of the search engine. This means the techniques that black hat SEO uses do not approve by the search engine. It is the opposite of White hat SEO.

Black Hat SEO includes the following techniques:

a) Cloaking: In this, the content of the search engine is different from the user browser.

b) Link Spam: This involves the posting of out of context links on the Website. 

c) Keyword Stuffing: This will manipulate the site ranking by entering the keywords.

d) Hidden texts and hidden links: In this technique, the spammer will hide the text and links on web pages for SERP rankings.


This method of Black Hat SEO will surely increase the ranking of your Website on a search engine in a short time but will definitely create a risk to your site. Google can ban your Website if it suspects anything wrong. So you should not choose this method for your Website.


What is Grey Hat Search Engine Optimization?

It is a mix of black hat and white hat SEO. You can say this technique uses black hat methods to achieve results in the white hat. It involves the ethical techniques that could one-day become black hat.
